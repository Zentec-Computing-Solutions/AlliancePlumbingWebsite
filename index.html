About

Local, dependable, and easy to reach.

Alliance Plumbing is the local plumbing, drainlaying, and gasfitting business based in Takaka. Also providing Hydrovaccing with safe and efficient service location.

Owned by Shane Harwood and run with Ben Clemens as the main operator, the team is set up for fast, dependable response times across Golden Bay.
